FuelImpact is a free web-based fuel cost calculator designed for users in India.

It helps users:
- Calculate daily, monthly, yearly fuel expenses
- Understand how fuel costs impact their income
- Make better financial decisions

Key Features:
- Fuel cost calculation
- Income percentage analysis
- Insight-based recommendations

Use cases:
- Personal finance planning
- Transportation cost estimation
- Budget optimization

Website: https://fuel-impact.vercel.app/
